# **Adding new names**

### Choosing the right path

From the front page of `WID`, navigate to the `Filter Cards` panel, on the left:
1. Click on `Nomenclature`; ciao 
2. Then on `New`

You will notice that both `Nomenclature` and `New` will be highlighted in green.

<img src="https://github.com/DavideDalPos/WID-manual/blob/1ca979a9511acae5ab25d6bcb9fe00c866ac1d80/img/NewTaxon_Filtercards.png" width="20%"/>

At this point, the central panel will show only 3 possible entries:
- `New combination`
- `New taxon name`
- `New type specimen`

Click on `New taxon name` (number 3 in the image below), and you will be directed to another page.

<img src="https://github.com/DavideDalPos/WID-manual/blob/1ca979a9511acae5ab25d6bcb9fe00c866ac1d80/img/NewTaxon_Centralpanel.png" width="100%"/>
